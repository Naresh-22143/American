About

Discover the Magic

of Storytelling

Welcome to American Picture House, where the art of storytelling takes center stage, and cinematic brilliance unfolds. We are the American Picture House Corporation, a dynamic force in film production, driven by an unwavering passion for creating captivating narratives that captivate and resonate.

At American Picture House, we embrace the transformative potential of cinema, recognizing its ability to inspire, provoke thought, and forge lasting connections with audiences. Our team comprises visionary filmmakers, writers, and industry professionals, united by a common goal: to produce films that not only entertain but also leave a lasting impact.

Leadership

Bannor Michael MacGregor

CEO/Chairperson


Mr. MacGregor has served as the Company’s Chief Executive Officer since September of 2017. Mr. MacGregor has been the Managing Member of Duncan Morgan, LLC since June of 2008. Prior to that, Mr. MacGregor served as the CEO and President of Bold Crayon Corporation from 2018 to 2022. Mr. MacGregor was a producer on the feature film, BUFFALOED (2020). Mr. MacGregor also served as an Executive Director at the Organization for the Advocacy of Multi Cultural Unity, Inc., a non-profit, that promotes cultural awareness, from 2016 to 2022.

Jonathan Sanger

President


Mr. Sanger is an acclaimed producer with a prolific career spanning over 40 years in both film and theater. His work has garnered significant recognition, including three Academy Awards, 21 Academy Award nominations, a British Academy Film Award, and a French César. Notable films he has produced include "The Elephant Man," "Frances," "Without Limits," "Vanilla Sky," and "The Producers—The Musical." Recently, he produced "Chapter & Verse" and "Marshall" and is completing "Cabrini." Sanger's directing credits include over 15 TV episodes and films like "Code Name Emerald" and "Down Came A Blackbird." He holds a BA and a Master's in Communications from the University of Pennsylvania and served in the Peace Corps, where he became fluent in Spanish.


Donald J. Harris, esq.

Secretary/Director


Mr. Harris has served as the Company’s Secretary since July of 2023. Mr. Harris serves as a partner at Harris Sarratt & Hodges, LLP since 1999. Mr. Harris is a corporate attorney and trial attorney specializing in complex business disputes involving issues related to corporate governance, and ownership, contracting, technology, intellectual property, and licensing.

Daniel Hirsch

Treasurer


Mr. Hirsch has served as the Company’s Treasurer since July of 2023. Prior to that Mr. Hirsch served as Chief Financial Officer and director of Todos Medical Ltd since January 5, 2020. Mr. Hirsch has been Managing Partner of First Line Capital, LLC since 2002. Mr. Hirsch holds a Bachelor’s Degree in Economics from Yeshiva University and a Master’s Degree in Public Health from the New School of Social Research.

James H. Hayne

CPA, Assistant Secretary and Assistant Treasurer

Mr. Hayne has served as the Company’s Assistant Secretary and Assistant Treasurer since July of 2023. Mr. Hayne is a finance executive with over 30 years’ corporate and public accounting experience. Mr. Hayne has been a shareholder at Hayne Financial Consulting, LLC, a consulting firm that principally provides contract accounting and chief financial officer services, since January 1, 2023. Mr. Hayne was a shareholder at Hayne, CPA, PLLC from November 1, 2017 through December 31, 2019 and from January 1, 2020 through December 31, 2022, Mr. Hayne was a shareholder with Dodson, Shelton & Nelson, P.A. Mr. Hayne holds a Bachelor’s Degree in Accounting from North Carolina State University and is a licensed Certified Public Accountant.


Michael “M.J.” Jones

Director of Business Affairs 

M.J. has over 25 years of experience in the film industry. He graduated from West Los Angeles College with an AA degree in Liberal Arts, focusing on Film Production. Jones began his career in casting, working on notable films such as "Set it Off," "Boyz in the Hood," and "Menace to Society," as well as television shows like "Hoops," "Living Single," and "Martin." After taking a break from the industry to start a family and pursue a successful career in the public sector, he returned to film as an Associate Producer on "Marshall," starring Chadwick Boseman, Kate Hudson, Josh Gad, Sterling K. Brown, and Dan Stevens. His latest project, "Cabrini," tells the story of the first American nun to be canonized and stars Christiana Dell'Anna.



History

We take pride in our commitment to excellence, evident in every phase of our productions – from the inception of compelling scripts to the meticulous details of post-production. Our embrace of diversity ensures that our films authentically reflect the multifaceted tapestry of human experiences, spanning genres from gripping dramas to heartwarming comedies.

Innovation is at the core of American Picture House. We eagerly adopt cutting-edge technologies and pioneering filmmaking techniques, ensuring that our audience is continually immersed in groundbreaking cinematic experiences. As the cinematic landscape evolves, so do we, staying ahead of the curve to deliver fresh, immersive, and unforgettable stories.

Beyond the silver screen, American Picture House Corporation fosters a collaborative and inclusive environment. We actively seek partnerships with emerging talents, industry leaders, and like-minded organizations to contribute to the dynamism and evolution of the film industry.

Embark on a cinematic journey with us, where creativity knows no bounds, and storytelling is elevated to an art form that transcends cultural and temporal boundaries. American Picture House invites you to immerse yourself in the magic of the movies, where every frame tells a unique story, leaving an indelible mark on the hearts and minds of our audiences.

Welcome to American Picture House – where the power of storytelling comes to life.
